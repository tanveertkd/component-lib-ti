Avatar

Avatars are usually used as or in place of profile pictures across websites. TI offers a couple of varieties of avatars in four different sizes.

  • Small - 24px*24px
  • Medium - 32px*32px
  • Normal - 48px*48px
  • Large - 64px*64px
You can style your avatars in the following ways.

Square Avatar

To use the square avatars, just add class avatar to your img tag. Please refer to the code snippet below for full usage information.
